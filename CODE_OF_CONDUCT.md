# Code of Conduct

10kmrr.life is an early macOS alpha project. Keep discussion practical, respectful, and focused on making the product safer and more useful for founders.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Assume good intent, but verify claims with concrete evidence when possible.
- Keep bug reports focused on reproducible behavior, environment details, and safe diagnostic summaries.
- Respect that alpha testers may be sharing feedback from their own business context.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing or requesting Stripe API keys, raw Stripe responses, raw logs, customer data, payment data, email/contact data, or exact private MRR.
- Posting unsanitized screenshots that expose private revenue, customer, invoice, payment, or account data.
- Pushing the project toward hosted Stripe key storage or full analytics dashboard scope without evidence from the alpha.

## Reporting

For public GitHub issues, include only sanitized summaries. Do not include secrets, exact MRR, raw logs, raw Stripe responses, customer data, payment data, email/contact data, or unsanitized screenshots.

For security-sensitive reports, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates these rules, especially content that exposes private business or payment data.

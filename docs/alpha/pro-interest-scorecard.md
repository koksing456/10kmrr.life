# Pro Interest Scorecard

Use this to evaluate whether Freemium + Pro has a real upgrade path.

Do not collect Stripe keys, customer/payment data, or unsanitized revenue screenshots.

Score only convenience and maintenance features that fit [free-pro-boundary.md](./free-pro-boundary.md).

| Tester | Custom design | Position/size controls | Refresh/cache controls | Multi-currency polish | MRR goal/delta/milestone | Signed/notarized installer | Compatibility updates | Preferred pricing | Overall Pro signal |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|---|
| Example Founder | 0-3 | 0-3 | 0-3 | 0-3 | 0-3 | 0-3 | 0-3 | one-time/monthly/annual/lifetime | none/low/medium/high |

## Scoring

- 0: no interest.
- 1: mild interest, would not pay alone.
- 2: clear interest, may influence upgrade.
- 3: strong paid pull.

## Strong Pro Signal

A tester counts as strong Pro signal if they:

- Score at least one feature as 3, and
- Name a preferred pricing model, and
- Say they would keep using the free alpha.

## Guardrail

If most Pro requests are full analytics features such as churn, cohort analysis, forecasting, or team reporting, treat that as a positioning risk rather than automatic scope expansion.

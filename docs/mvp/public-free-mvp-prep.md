# Public Free MVP Prep

## Goal

Prepare a public-facing Free MVP while keeping actual app installation gated to a small private alpha.

## Public Promise

Your Stripe MRR, on your Mac Lock Screen.

## Target User

Mac-first indie hackers and solo SaaS founders who use Stripe subscriptions and want their MRR visible every day without opening a full analytics dashboard.

### Why This Exists

Stripe Dashboard and subscription analytics tools are useful for reporting. 10kmrr.life is for the moment you open your Mac and want your core revenue signal in front of you.

### Free Alpha

The free alpha focuses on one job:

- Show your current Stripe MRR on your Mac Lock Screen.

It intentionally does not replace Baremetrics, ChartMogul, Stripe Dashboard, or finance reporting tools.

### Local And Private

- Restricted read-only Stripe key.
- Stored locally in macOS Keychain.
- MRR cache stored locally.
- No server-side storage of your Stripe key or revenue data in the current alpha.

### Compatibility Note

The lock-screen overlay uses private macOS behavior. It is a personal Mac tool first, and future macOS releases may require fixes.

### Pro Interest

Ask users which Pro upgrades they would care about:

- Custom lock-screen design.
- Refresh and cache controls.
- Multi-currency display polish.
- MRR goal / milestone / delta.
- Signed / notarized distribution and ongoing macOS compatibility.
